## Additional Verbs

### U-Verbs

- 会\[あ\]う - to meet (に)

- 買\[か\]う - to buy (を)

- 書\[か\]く - to write (が)

- 撮\[と\]る - to take (a picture)

- 待\[ま\]つ - to wait (を)

- ある - there is (が)

- わかる - to understand (が)

### RU-Verbs

- いる - (a person) is in..., stay at... (place に; person が)
